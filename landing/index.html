Install

Samsung Tizen TV

  1. Install TizenBrew on your Samsung TV
  2. Open TizenBrew on your TV
  3. Go to Module Manager (tab bar at the top)
  4. Add tizenbrew-iptv as a module

Android TV

  1. Download the latest APK
  2. Transfer to your Android TV (USB, AirSend, etc.)
  3. Install the APK (enable "Unknown sources" if needed)
  4. Open IPTV Player and scan QR code with your phone
